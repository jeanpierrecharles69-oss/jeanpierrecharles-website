## 3. OPERATIONAL METHODOLOGY (HOW WE THINK)

### A. Context Saturation & Curation (Kaizen)

* **Principle**: Context is finite. Do not pollute it.
* **Action**: Before adding information, ask "Does this serve the 2050 Vision?" and "Is it already in a Master File?".
* **Convergence**: Merge similar topics immediately. Delete obsolete branches.

### B. Causal Reasoning (Judea Pearl)

* **Level 1 (Association)**: "What does the data say?" (Diagnostic)
* **Level 2 (Intervention)**: "What happens if we do X?" (Prescriptive)
* **Level 3 (Counterfactual)**: "What would have happened if...?" (Strategic)
* **Usage**: All strategic decisions must pass the Counterfactual test.

### C. Hermeneutic Analysis

* **The Loop**: Understand the *Part* (e.g., a specific AI Act article) to understand the *Whole* (the Project Vision), and vice-versa.
* **Application**: Never code a feature without understanding its regulatory impact. Never cite a regulation without understanding its industrial impact.

### D. Bounded Rationality & Human Factor (Herbert Simon)

* **Concept**: Humans are limited by information, time, and cognitive processing power. This creates **Cognitive Barriers** to optimal compliance.
* **Strategy**: Instead of seeking impossible "Optimization", we aim for **Satisficing** (meeting critical requirements efficiently).
* **Application**: The Aegis Platform acts as a **Cognitive Augmentation** tool to bridge the gap between complex EU Law and the limited "mental bandwidth" of the industrial operator.

### E. Protocol A-ECR (Aegis Engineering Change Request)

Inspired by industrial PLM (Product Lifecycle Management), this procedure **MUST** be initiated before any significant change or new conversation thread.

#### 1. Identification (The Input)

* **Definition**: Clear description of the "Problem" (current state) and the "Target" (desired state).

#### 2. Impact Analysis (The Matrix Check)

Evaluate the change against the 5 Master Files (The Digital Data Package):

* **Business Impact** (`PRJ_BUSINESS_STRATEGY`): Does it affect MRR, Tiers, or the Outre-Mer bridge?
* **Technical Impact** (`PRJ_TECHNICAL_CORE`): Does it affect Determinism, Deterministic Config, or the RAG Architecture?
* **Regulatory Impact** (`PRJ_COMPLIANCE_MATRIX`): Does it touch AI Act, ESPR, or CRA? Is it still compliant?
* **History Impact** (`PRJ_PROJECT_HISTORY`): Does it conflict with a previous DEC (Decision)?

#### 3. Causal Assessment (Pearl Level 2/3)

* **Intervention**: "If we implement this change, what is the immediate metric of success?"
* **Counterfactual**: "What happens if we DON'T do it? (Cost of Inaction)".

#### 4. V&V Plan (Verification & Validation)

* **V-Gate**: Define the pass/fail criteria for this change.
* **Approval**: Change is only "Production-Ready" once the AI validates all 5 matrices.
